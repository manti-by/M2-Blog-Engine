Information for rights holders

If you are the copyright owner of any material, the link (or links) to which is posted on this site, and would not want this information to be distributed by users without your consent, we will be happy to assist you by removing the appropriate links.

For this it is necessary that you send us a letter (in electronic form) in which you indicated to us the following information:

  1. Documentary evidence of your rights to material protected by copyright:
    • A scanned document with a print, or
    • Email from the company's official email domain, or
    • Other contact information that allows you to uniquely identify you as the copyright owner of this material.
  2. The text that you wish to place with the deleted information. In it you can specify where and under what conditions you can get the information, the links to which have been deleted, as well as your contact information, so that users can get from you all the information they are interested in regarding this material.
  3. Direct links to the pages of the site that contain links to the data that you want to delete. Links should be in the form https://manti.by/xxxx/ or similar.
  4. After that, within 48 hours, we will remove the links you are interested in from the site.

ATTENTION!!!

We reserve the right to publish on the website any information sent to us by mail through the feedback form.

We do not monitor the actions of users who can re-post links to information that is subject to your copyright. In any case, we will consider all your requests regarding references to information that violates your rights.

According to the Law on Copyright and Neighboring Rights, the reference to any data (information message), in itself, is not subject to copyright. Thus, it is not necessary to send letters containing threats or demands, as having no real basis.
